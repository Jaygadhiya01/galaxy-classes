Who We Are

A Decade of Shaping Futures

Galaxy Classes was established in 2014 with a clear mission: to provide quality, affordable, and result-oriented coaching to students across Ahmedabad. What started as a small tutorial class has grown into a trusted educational institution serving 500+ students every year.

We believe every student has the potential to excel — they simply need the right guidance, environment, and encouragement. Our experienced faculty, structured curriculum, and student-first approach have consistently delivered outstanding board results.

Our Approach

The Galaxy Classes Method

We follow a student-centred, concept-first approach that goes beyond textbooks.

💡
Concept-Based Learning
We focus on building deep conceptual understanding rather than just memorizing answers. Students learn the "why" behind every topic so they can solve any question, seen or unseen.
👤
Personal Attention
Our small batch sizes (max 15–20 students) ensure every child gets individual attention. Teachers know each student's strengths, weaknesses, and learning pace personally.
📝
Regular Tests
Weekly chapter tests, monthly unit tests, and pre-board mock exams keep students in constant revision mode and eliminate last-minute exam stress completely.
📊
Progress Tracking
Detailed performance reports after every test. Parents receive regular WhatsApp and email updates about their child's academic progress and areas to improve.
💬
Doubt Sessions
Separate doubt-clearing sessions ensure no concept is left unclear. Students can ask questions freely without hesitation in our supportive environment.
🏆
Exam Strategies
Beyond academics, we teach time management, smart answering techniques, paper presentation, and mental preparation for high-pressure exams.
