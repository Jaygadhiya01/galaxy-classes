FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Galaxy Classes.

What standards do you offer coaching for? +
We offer comprehensive coaching for students from Standard 1 to Standard 12, covering both Science and Commerce streams in English and Gujarati Medium.
What is the batch size at Galaxy Classes? +
We maintain small batch sizes of maximum 15–20 students per batch. This ensures every student receives personal attention and no child is left behind.
Do you offer a demo class before admission? +
Yes! All new students are welcome to attend a free demo class before confirming admission. This helps students and parents experience our teaching quality and classroom environment.
Do you provide coaching in Gujarati Medium? +
Absolutely! We provide coaching in both English and Gujarati Medium. Students can choose the medium they are most comfortable with for better understanding of concepts.
How do you keep parents informed about progress? +
We conduct regular Parent-Teacher meetings and share weekly test results directly with parents via WhatsApp. Monthly progress reports are also shared covering performance trends and improvement areas.
